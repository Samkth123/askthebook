About Ask the Holy Book

Ask the Holy Book helps you explore Bible, Quran, and Torah passages with references and respectful interpretation.

How answers are built

  • We retrieve relevant passages from the selected book.
  • We summarize and interpret with citations for chapter and verse.
  • We include modern context and scholarly perspectives when possible.

Current events

  • If web search results are available, we use them and cite sources.
  • If search isn’t available, we answer from general background knowledge and the texts.
  • We avoid definitive claims about very recent developments without sources.

Compare mode

  • Ask one question and receive side-by-side answers.
  • See similarities and differences across traditions.
  • Toggle books to refine the comparison.

Limitations & care

  • This is informational, not a substitute for clergy or scholarly guidance.
  • Interpretations can vary by tradition and community.
  • We aim to be respectful and careful with sensitive topics.
