About Ask the Holy Book

Ask the Holy Book helps you explore the Qur'an, Bible, and Torah with references and careful context.

What we provide

  • Clear answers grounded in passages from the selected text.
  • Citations for chapter and verse.
  • Context and interpretive notes, written respectfully.

Current events

  • We separate textual principles, factual reporting, and interpretive bridges.
  • We cite sources when reporting current events.
  • We avoid definitive claims without sources.

Limitations & care

  • This is informational, not a substitute for clergy or scholarly guidance.
  • Interpretations vary by tradition and community.
  • We aim to be careful with sensitive topics.
