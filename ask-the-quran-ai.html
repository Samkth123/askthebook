What is Ask the Quran AI?

Ask the Quran AI is an advanced artificial intelligence tool designed to answer questions about the Holy Quran. Whether you're studying Islamic theology, exploring ethical questions, or seeking guidance on current events, this Quran AI provides clear, cited answers from scripture.

When you ask the Quran AI a question, the system uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to find the most relevant passages from the Quran's surahs. It then provides a comprehensive answer with specific citations like "Al-Fatiha 1:1-7" or "Al-Baqarah 2:255," along with context and interpretation.

Unlike generic chatbots, ask the Quran AI is specifically trained on Quranic texts and their contexts. It understands the difference between different surahs, recognizes historical context, and can explain how various passages relate to your question.
