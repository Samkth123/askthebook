What is Ask the Bible and Quran Compare?

Ask the Bible and Quran compare is a powerful comparison tool that lets you see how the Christian Bible and the Holy Quran address the same question. When you ask the Bible and Quran compare a question, you receive side-by-side answers with citations from both texts.

This comparison feature is invaluable for interfaith dialogue, academic study, and personal exploration. It reveals both shared principles and distinct perspectives, helping you understand how these two major religious traditions approach similar questions.

Whether you're studying theology, exploring ethical questions, or seeking to understand different perspectives on current events, ask the Bible and Quran compare provides clear, cited answers from both traditions.

How to Use Ask the Bible and Quran Compare

  1. Type your question in the search box
  2. Select both "Bible" and "Quran" as your sources
  3. Click "Ask" to receive side-by-side answers
  4. Review citations from both texts (Bible: chapter:verse, Quran: surah:verse)

The comparison tool shows you exactly how each text addresses your question, making it easy to see similarities and differences. Each answer includes specific citations so you can verify the passages yourself.

What You Get When You Compare Bible and Quran

  • Side-by-side answers from both Bible and Quran
  • Chapter-and-verse citations from the Bible (e.g., John 3:16, Matthew 5:3-12)
  • Surah and verse citations from the Quran (e.g., Al-Fatiha 1:1-7, Al-Baqarah 2:255)
  • Context and interpretation for each tradition
  • Clear identification of shared principles and distinct perspectives
