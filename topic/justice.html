Justice

How each text approaches justice

Qur'an

Justice is tied to God-consciousness, fair dealing, and protection of the oppressed.

Bible

Justice emphasizes integrity, mercy, and defending the widow, orphan, and stranger.

Torah

Justice requires fair courts, honest measures, and care for those at the margins.

FAQ

Is justice the same as punishment?

Across the texts, justice includes accountability, but also mercy, restoration, and truth-telling.

Do the texts allow biased judgment?

No. Each tradition emphasizes impartiality and protection of the vulnerable.
