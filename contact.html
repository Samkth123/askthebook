How to Reach Us

You can contact Ask the Holy Book for the following purposes:

Corrections and Factual Errors

If you identify an error in citation, historical information, or interpretation, please contact us with:

  • The page URL where the error appears
  • The specific error or incorrect information
  • The correct information with source citation

We review all correction requests and update content promptly when errors are verified.

Content Suggestions

We welcome suggestions for:

  • New article topics
  • Expansions to existing articles
  • Additional perspectives to include
  • Topics for comparative analysis

Feedback on AI Responses

If you encounter an AI-generated response that is inaccurate, incomplete, or lacks proper citations, please report it with:

  • The question asked
  • The response received
  • What was incorrect or missing

This helps us improve our AI system's accuracy.

General Questions

For questions about our methodology, editorial policy, or how to use the platform, please contact us. We aim to respond within 3-5 business days.

Contact Form

Please use the form below to contact us. All fields are required.

Anti-Spam Policy

We use automated systems to filter spam and unsolicited commercial messages. To ensure your message is received:

  • Use a valid email address
  • Provide clear, specific information
  • Avoid promotional language or sales pitches
  • Focus on content-related inquiries

Messages that appear to be spam, contain promotional content, or violate our terms of service will be filtered and may not receive a response.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all legitimate inquiries within 3-5 business days. Correction requests are prioritized and typically addressed within 1-2 business days.

Privacy

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not share your contact information with third parties. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
