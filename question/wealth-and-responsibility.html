Question

How do the scriptures speak about wealth and responsibility?

A comparative summary of generosity, stewardship, and care for those in need across the Qur'an, Bible, and Torah.

Sources: Qur'an, Bible, Torah Focus: generosity
Answer

1. Textual principles

The Qur'an emphasizes charity, honesty in trade, and responsibility to the poor. The Bible warns against greed and urges generosity. The Torah requires care for the vulnerable through fair labor, tithes, and gleaning laws.

Citations: Qur'an 2:177 · Proverbs 11:25 · Deuteronomy 24:19-22

2. Current facts (when applicable)

When connecting to current economic debates, we separate verified data from textual interpretation.

3. Bridging to the present

A text-informed perspective prioritizes stewardship, transparency, and care for those most affected by inequality.
