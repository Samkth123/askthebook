Question

What do the scriptures teach about forgiveness after conflict?

A careful look at mercy, accountability, and reconciliation across the Qur'an, Bible, and Torah.

Sources: Qur'an, Bible, Torah Focus: reconciliation
Answer

1. Textual principles

The texts praise forgiveness but do not erase justice. The Qur'an values mercy while permitting measured accountability. The Bible highlights reconciliation and love of neighbor. The Torah emphasizes restoring relationships and repairing harm.

Citations: Qur'an 24:22 · Matthew 6:14-15 · Leviticus 19:18

2. Current facts (when applicable)

When applying these principles to real conflicts, we separate verified facts from interpretation and cite sources for events.

3. Bridging to the present

A text-informed approach often encourages truth-telling, restitution, and long-term reconciliation, while avoiding calls for revenge.
