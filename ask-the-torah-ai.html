What is Ask the Torah AI?

Ask the Torah AI is an advanced artificial intelligence tool designed to answer questions about the Torah—the first five books of the Hebrew Bible: Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, and Deuteronomy. Whether you're studying Jewish theology, exploring ethical questions, or seeking guidance on current events, this Torah AI provides clear, cited answers from scripture.

When you ask the Torah AI a question, the system uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to find the most relevant passages from these foundational texts. It then provides a comprehensive answer with specific citations like "Genesis 1:1" or "Exodus 20:1-17," along with context and interpretation.

Unlike generic chatbots, ask the Torah AI is specifically trained on Torah texts and their contexts. It understands the difference between different books, recognizes historical context, and can explain how various passages relate to your question.
